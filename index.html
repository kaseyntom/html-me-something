Living a Healthy lifestyle.

Went for my annual physical. All tests came back as:

  • Cholesterol, a little high
  • Weight, a little high
  • Blood sugar, a little high

So I asked the Dr, "What can I do to avoid taking medication?"


  • Eat more fruits and vegtables
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes 3 times a week
  • Get plenty of rest
  • Drink 8 glasses of water a day

To avoid taking medicine and improve my health, I decided to train for a marathon

  • Things I learned the hard way
    • Proper footwear, best to be fitted from a running store. Just because it says Nike on your shoe doesn't make it right for your feet.
    • Must give your feet a rest. Train every other day.
    • Drink drink drink and mostly water
    • Stretching will avoid many injuries

    So a bone spur and bunion later, I decided what can I do different that would not pound one part of my body. And I came up with triathlons.

    />
  • What I like about triathlons
    • Always with a group, which keeps you competitive.
    • Not pounding the same part of your body. Total body workout.
    • When you finish, it is a sense of accomplishment.
